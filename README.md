📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Coffee Sales Analytics Dashboard developed in Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights, helping stakeholders monitor sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and store-wise comparisons.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance.
Track Month-over-Month (MoM) growth.
Identify top-selling coffee products.
Analyze hourly sales trends.
Compare sales across multiple store locations.
Evaluate product category and cup size performance.
📊 Dashboard Features
💰 Total Sales KPI
🛒 Total Orders KPI
📦 Total Quantity Sold
💵 Average Order Value
🏪 Total Stores
☕ Total Products
📈 Monthly Sales Trend
🥇 Top 5 Selling Coffee Products
⏰ Sales by Hour
🥤 Sales by Cup Size
📦 Sales by Product Category & Store
📊 Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth
🎛️ Interactive Store Filters
📈 Key Insights
Coffee is the highest revenue-generating product category.
Tea is the second-best performing category.
Peak sales occur during the morning hours.
Sales show a positive month-over-month growth trend.
Store-wise comparison helps identify the best-performing outlet.
Cup size analysis reveals customer purchasing preferences.
